DealSense AI Sales Benchmarks v1.0

Sales Performance Benchmarks

Average Win Rate
- Enterprise: 32%
- Mid-Market: 41%
- SMB: 53%

Customer Activity Benchmarks
- High-performing deals average one customer interaction every 5 days.
- Opportunities with more than 21 days of inactivity have a significantly lower win rate.
- Deals with documented next steps after every customer meeting close faster.

Sales Stage Benchmarks
- Qualification: 7–14 days
- Discovery: 10–20 days
- Proposal: 15–30 days
- Negotiation: 10–20 days

Risk Indicators
- Opportunity remains in one stage for more than 45 days.
- Probability below 20% after Discovery.
- No executive sponsor identified.
- Multiple close date extensions.

Positive Indicators
- Executive sponsor actively engaged.
- Procurement involved early.
- Security review completed before negotiation.
- Recent customer meetings with documented action items.
