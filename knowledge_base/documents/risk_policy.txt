DealSense AI Risk Policy v1.0

High Risk Criteria
- No customer activity for more than 21 days.
- Opportunity has remained in the same stage for more than 45 days.
- Opportunity close date has already passed.
- Customer has not committed to a clear next step.

Medium Risk Criteria
- Low probability (<20%) outside Prospecting or Qualification.
- Missing opportunity description.
- Missing executive sponsor.

Low Risk Signals
- Customer activity within the last 3 days.
- Opportunity is in Negotiation with recent customer engagement.
- Clearly defined next steps.

Human Review Required
- Deal value greater than or equal to $500,000.
- Strategic enterprise customer.
- Exception requests requiring VP approval.

Business Rule
Guardrail rules take precedence over AI recommendations. The AI may explain the reasoning but must not override a critical guardrail.
